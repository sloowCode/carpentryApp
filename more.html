WHY CHOOSE US

Our Mission

We are highly skilled, efficient and cluey carpenters with the highest attention to detail and impressive creative design skills. We are honest, punctual, reliable and professional. With over 20 years experience in ALL aspects of building and carpentry; we can cater to all your carpentry/building, home maintenance and renovation needs. We stock a large range of precision tools and accessories to achieve that perfect job, first time, every time.

Our Vision

We insist on high quality and well thought out finishes on all our jobs big and small, for an affordable price, we also understand the imposition renovations can be on the household/business, and the importance of getting back to normal so we aim to be in and out as quick and tidy as possible for you and your family during these times. Your needs is our priority.

Strategic Goals

We understand the importance of being under budget and we can adapt an approach to better suit your position. We also have close relationships with other trades (electrician/plumber/painter etc) so we can take care of every aspect of the work from concept to completion, so you can relax and not worry about a thing!
